Industry & Organization

Industry: Retail / E-commerce


Organization:  Medium-sized retail company selling consumer products across multiple categories and regions

Current State:  Company records daily sales transactions but lacks structured data analysis capabilities

Objective:  Better understand sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and use historical data for forecasting and planning


Business Problem :

Despite having substantial sales data, the company faces several challenges:

Lack of centralized, structured view of sales performance

Difficulty identifying top-performing products, customers, and regions

Limited visibility into seasonal patterns and sales trends

Weak data-driven support for sales forecasting and planning

Inability to understand customer segment behavior effectively


Business Objectives

The BI solution aims to:


Monitor overall sales revenue performance

Identify top-performing and underperforming products, categories, and regions

Analyze customer purchasing behavior by segment

Track sales trends over time (monthly, quarterly, yearly)

Support sales forecasting and planning activities
